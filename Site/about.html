About Us

The story behind the cabin

Small Studio, Big Heart

Tiny Cabin Creative was born from a simple belief: the best digital experiences feel like home. Not flashy or overwhelming, but warm, welcoming, and thoughtfully crafted. We're a small studio that believes in quality over quantity, relationships over transactions, and building websites that actually serve the people who use them.

Every project we take on is an opportunity to create something meaningful. We don't work with just anyone—we partner with businesses and individuals who share our values of authenticity, sustainability, and genuine connection with their audience. Whether you're a local coffee roaster, a wellness practitioner, or a maker with a story to tell, we're here to help you share it with the world.

Our Philosophy

We believe great design isn't about following trends—it's about understanding people. Before we write a single line of code or push a pixel, we take time to understand your business, your audience, and your goals. We ask questions, listen deeply, and then craft solutions that are as unique as you are.

Our approach is rooted in collaboration. You're the expert in your business, and we're experts in translating that expertise into digital experiences that work. We don't believe in talking over your head with jargon or disappearing for weeks at a time. Throughout the entire process, you'll know exactly what we're building and why.

Sustainability matters to us, too. We build websites that are fast, efficient, and easy to maintain. No bloated code, no unnecessary complexity—just clean, purposeful solutions that will serve you well for years to come.

Meet the Team

Tiny Cabin Creative is led by a designer and developer who left the corporate world to build a more intentional practice. After years of creating digital experiences for big brands, the desire to work more closely with small businesses and creative entrepreneurs became impossible to ignore.

What started as a side project in a literal tiny cabin in the woods has grown into a boutique studio serving clients across the country. Despite the growth, we've kept our values intact: every project gets personal attention, every client becomes a partner, and every website is built with care.

When we're not designing and coding, you'll find us hiking local trails, experimenting with new recipes, or getting lost in a good book. We believe the best creative work comes from a balanced life, and we bring that same philosophy to our partnerships with clients.
