E-commerce

Artisan Goods Marketplace

A beautiful online storefront connecting local artisans with conscious consumers

Project Overview

We partnered with a collective of local artisans to create an e-commerce platform that celebrates handcrafted goods. The challenge was to build a marketplace that felt personal and authentic while providing robust shopping functionality.

What We Delivered

  • Custom WordPress e-commerce site with WooCommerce
  • Individual artisan profile pages with storytelling
  • Advanced filtering and search capabilities
  • Mobile-optimized shopping experience
  • Integrated inventory management system

Results

The platform launched with 25 artisans and grew to over 60 within six months. Mobile sales increased by 145%, and average time on site improved by 3 minutes.

Timeline 12 weeks
